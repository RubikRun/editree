Version 0.01 of editree

Version 0.01 is just a basic video player which plays a hardcoded video.
It consists of:
1. Rectangular area where the video is played, or a paused frame is shown
2. Play/pause button
3. Button for going to the beggining of the video
4. Timeline with a movable playhead
    4.1. Playhead moves with the progress of the video
    4.2. When user clicks on the timeline the playhead goes to where they clicked moving the video's progress accordingly
